About the Kumasi Campus

The Nursing Students Association of Valley View University, Kumasi Campus, operates within a serene academic environment designed to promote excellence in nursing education, leadership, and professional development.

The campus provides modern lecture halls, clinical training facilities, laboratories, and student-centered learning spaces that support hands-on healthcare education and research.

Located in the Ashanti Region of Ghana, the Kumasi Campus serves as a hub for academic growth, community outreach, and professional nursing advancement.
