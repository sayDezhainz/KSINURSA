About Kumasi Campus

Who We Are

The Nursing Students’ Association (NSA–VVU Kumasi Campus) represents all nursing students of Valley View University, Kumasi Campus. We exist to promote unity, professionalism, academic growth, and ethical nursing practice among our members.

Our students graduate prepared to thrive in a global world while serving their communities with integrity.
